# MarketWatch: AI Intelligence Team

> AI-powered competitive intelligence platform that monitors competitors, customers, and industry trends in real time.

---

## 📖 Overview

**MarketWatch** is a multi-agent AI system designed to help businesses stay ahead of the competition by continuously gathering, analyzing, and synthesizing market intelligence.

The platform transforms raw market data into actionable insights through specialized AI agents focused on marketing, product development, sales, and strategic decision-making.

---

## AI Agents

### Marketing AI
- Monitors competitor advertisements and campaigns
- Tracks marketing trends and brand activities
- Identifies emerging opportunities and threats

### Product AI
- Analyzes customer reviews and feedback
- Detects feature requests and product gaps
- Performs sentiment analysis across platforms

### Sales AI
- Identifies buying signals and lead opportunities
- Tracks customer demand patterns
- Highlights potential market segments

###  Strategy AI
- Combines insights from all AI agents
- Generates actionable business recommendations
- Supports data-driven strategic planning
